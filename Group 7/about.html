About Us

We are a Pizza restaurant based in Parklands area, Nairobi. We are dedicated to making your best pizzas and customer satisfaction is our number one priority. In regards to this, we take much care and consideration in how we prepare our meals so as to ensure we are servingyou in the best and healthiest way possible. Hygiene is paramount and you can be assured that our food is prepared in one of the cleanest environments. To add to this, we are approved by the Halal Board of Kenya.
